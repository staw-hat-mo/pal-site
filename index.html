Historical Background

The Israel–Palestine conflict developed over time through political, territorial, and historical developments.

Late 1800s–Early 1900s: Rise of competing national movements in the region.
1917–1948: British Mandate period; increasing tensions between communities.
1948: Arab–Israeli war and large-scale displacement of Palestinians (Nakba).
1967: Six-Day War; major territorial changes in the region.
2000s–Present: Repeated cycles of conflict, negotiations, and escalations.
